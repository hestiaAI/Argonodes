argonodes.models module

Models are an abstraction of data sources.

They aim to describe, complete, and enhance the information of unknown data, so that the information related to the data can be re-applied to new data.

argonodes.nodes module

Nodes are the building block of the whole pipeline to augment data with semantics.

With Nodes, data can be passed in JSON, which can be explored, described, and tagged, and the nodes can then be used to build data models.
